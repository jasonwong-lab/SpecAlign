View menu commands

The View menu offers the following commands:

Toolbar Shows or hides the toolbar.
Status Bar Shows or hides the status bar.
Average Spectrum Shows or hides the average spectrum.
Baseline Shows (picks) or hides (removes) the baseline.
View As Give options to toggle between the type of graph the spectra are displayed as.
Auto scale Automatically scales all spectra to fit its window
Fastdraw Speeds up drawing of spectra on screen by drawing only a fraction of the data points.
